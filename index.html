Welcome!

I am a Machine Learning Engineer at Abstract Security, and a CS MSc student at USC. Previously, I received my BS in Physics from UCR. My main interests are in probabilistic modeling, causal inference and more recently applied NLP/LLMs.

Before joining Abstract, I worked on Causal RL at USC, developing safe transfer learning methods for agents. I also had the opportunity to contribute to interdisciplinary data science research at the ISI, including Social Network Analysis and Geology. Even before that, I helped analyze X-ray tomography data from the Advanced Light Source for NASA Entry Systems Parachutes. My research journey started off as a Physics student in Barsukov lab, working on micromagnetic magnetic simulations.

In my free time, I enjoy challenging for Local Legends/KOMs on Strava, attending music festivals and traveling!
